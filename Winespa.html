Everybody deserves to relax and unwind from time to time. There are probably very few better ways to do it than taking this amazing and intoxicating tour, travelling from the East to the West of Georgia. Take the amazing Wine & Spa tour and feel like a Georgian aristocrat in the opulent chateaus, enjoying healing spas and wine cellars. You will visit Kakheti, the famous wine region with beautiful nature, ancient monasteries and 8000 years-old traditional method of wine production which is still being used by the local families and wineries, experience variety of spa treatments, including bath in Saperavi wine and in unique mineral springs in once elite Soviet bathhouses of Tskaltubo. The tour includes visits to several UNESCO World Heritage sites.

Tour Plan
Day 1: Urban beauty of Tbilisi Pick-up from Tbilisi airport, accommodation at 4* hotel in Tbilisi, walking tour and city exploration, tasting the best out of Georgian cuisine.
Day 2: Wine Spa Travelling to Kakheti region, accommodation in Schumann Wine & Spa resort, enjoying the spa procedures, including the Saperavi wine baths. Wine degustation.
Day 3: Exploring Glorious Past of Georgia Trip to Tsinandali, visiting Chavchavadze home museum - 18th century Georgian noble family house, transportation to Gremi Monastery and lunch at TEMI community and social enterprise that produces organic food and wines. Visiting Nekresi, one of the oldest monasteries in Georgia (4th century). Dinner and city tour in Telavi, a picturesque town with a beautiful view of Alazani valley.
Day 4: Aristocratic Day Visit to Mtskheta, the ancient capital of Georgia and Jvari Monastery. Lunch and wine degustation at Chateau Mukhrani, built by the members of the Georgian royal family in 1873 with a long tradition in winemaking, visiting the vineyards, and the castle. Accommodation at the 4* hotel next to Bazaleti lake.
Day 5: Tskaltubo - healing waters and abandoned bathhouses Transportation to Tskaltubo, former elite Soviet spa resorts with Stalin’s favourite bathhouse. Nowadays several of the sanatoriums are being renewed as premium class hotels and spas. Enjoying spa procedures and walk around the city.
Day 6: Exploring Western Georgia Trip to Prometheus Cave, lunch and wine degustation in a local winery with organic food and wines, small hike between Motsameta and Gelati monasteries (UNESCO), sightseeing in Kutaisi. Accommodation in Tskaltubo.
Day 7: Happy Ending Travelling back to Tbilisi. Free time in the city, sightseeing. Farewell dinner - Traditional Georgian Supra with Georgian dances performance.
