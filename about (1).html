About

Who is Pilpilio?

We are a young team that has a simple goal: To show you the best out of Georgia. We will introduce you its beautiful nature, rich culture, share the tales about its past and help you to understand the most recent development or even find investment and business opportunities. We have lived in various places, travelled the world, seen numerous of its wonders, hiked dozens of mountains and worked with many international groups. Then we decided to come back to Georgia and design and curate beautiful experiences for those who are coming to see our country. We know the best of what our country has to offer. We are ready to experiment, offer you an adventure and a peek to the backstage, behind the usual tourist facades. At the same time, we recognise the highest standards of the services that we offer to our clients. We take the responsibility, so you can relax and enjoy.
